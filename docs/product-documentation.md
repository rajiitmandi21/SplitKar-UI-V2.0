# SplitKar Product Documentation

## 🎯 Product Overview

SplitKar is a modern, user-friendly expense splitting application designed to eliminate the hassle of managing shared expenses among friends, family, and colleagues. Whether you're planning a group trip, sharing household expenses, or organizing a dinner party, SplitKar makes it easy to track who paid what and who owes whom.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 💰 Smart Expense Splitting
- **Multiple Split Methods**: Equal splits, custom amounts, percentages, or itemized splitting
- **Real-time Calculations**: Instant balance updates as expenses are added
- **Flexible Participation**: Include or exclude specific group members from individual expenses
- **Receipt Management**: Upload and store receipt images for expense verification

### 👥 Group Management
- **Easy Group Creation**: Set up groups for different activities or ongoing shared expenses
- **Member Invitations**: Invite friends via email or phone number
- **Role-based Access**: Group creators and admins can manage settings and members
- **Multiple Groups**: Participate in unlimited groups simultaneously

### 🔄 Settlement Tracking
- **Debt Optimization**: Smart algorithms minimize the number of transactions needed
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Support for UPI, bank transfers, cash, and digital wallets
- **Settlement Confirmation**: Both parties confirm payments for transparency
- **Payment History**: Complete record of all settlements and transactions

### 📊 Analytics & Insights
- **Spending Analytics**: Understand your spending patterns by category and time
- **Group Insights**: See group spending trends and individual contributions
- **Export Options**: Download expense reports in PDF or CSV format
- **Budget Tracking**: Set and monitor group budgets (coming soon)

### 🔔 Smart Notifications
- **Real-time Updates**: Instant notifications for new expenses and settlements
- **Payment Reminders**: Gentle reminders for outstanding debts
- **Customizable Alerts**: Choose which notifications you want to receive
- **Multi-channel Delivery**: In-app, email, and push notifications

## 👤 User Personas & Use Cases

### The Trip Organizer
**Sarah, 28, Marketing Manager**

Sarah frequently organizes weekend trips and group activities with her friends. She's tired of manually tracking expenses on spreadsheets and chasing people for money after trips.

**How SplitKar Helps:**
- Creates a dedicated group for each trip
- Adds expenses in real-time during the trip
- Automatically calculates who owes what
- Sends payment reminders after the trip
- Keeps a record for future reference

**Typical Workflow:**
1. Creates "Goa Weekend Trip" group
2. Invites 6 friends to the group
3. Adds hotel booking expense (₹12,000) split equally
4. Adds dinner expenses with custom splits based on what people ordered
5. Reviews final balances and sends settlement requests

### The Roommate
**Mike, 24, Software Developer**

Mike shares an apartment with 3 roommates and they split utilities, groceries, and household expenses. He wants a simple way to track ongoing shared expenses.

**How SplitKar Helps:**
- Maintains a "Apartment 4B" group for all roommates
- Tracks monthly recurring expenses like utilities
- Handles grocery shopping splits
- Manages security deposit and maintenance costs
- Provides monthly spending summaries

**Typical Workflow:**
1. Adds monthly electricity bill (₹2,400) split equally among 4 roommates
2. Records grocery shopping with itemized splits
3. Handles one-time expenses like furniture purchases
4. Reviews monthly balance and settles up

### The Budget-Conscious User
**Lisa, 32, Financial Analyst**

Lisa carefully tracks her personal finances and wants detailed insights into her group spending. She needs categorized expenses and spending analytics.

**How SplitKar Helps:**
- Categorizes all expenses for better tracking
- Provides detailed spending analytics
- Exports data for personal budgeting tools
- Shows spending trends over time
- Helps identify areas to reduce group spending

**Typical Workflow:**
1. Reviews monthly spending by category
2. Analyzes group vs. personal expenses
3. Exports data to Excel for budget planning
4. Sets spending alerts for group activities
5. Tracks progress against personal budget goals

### The Frequent Traveler
**David, 35, Consultant**

David travels frequently for work and leisure, often with different groups of people. He needs to handle complex expense scenarios and multiple currencies.

**How SplitKar Helps:**
- Manages multiple travel groups simultaneously
- Handles complex splitting scenarios
- Stores receipt images for expense reports

- Provides detailed trip summaries

**Typical Workflow:**
1. Creates separate groups for work trips and personal travel

## 🎯 Competitive Advantage

### Unique Value Propositions

#### 1. Simplicity Without Compromise
While competitors often overwhelm users with features, SplitKar focuses on making expense splitting as simple as possible without sacrificing functionality.

#### 2. Indian Market Focus
Built specifically for the Indian market with support for UPI payments, Indian banking systems, and local user preferences.

#### 3. Smart Debt Optimization
Advanced algorithms minimize the number of transactions needed to settle all debts within a group, saving time and reducing complexity.

#### 4. Real-time Collaboration
Unlike static expense tracking apps, SplitKar provides real-time updates and notifications, making it feel like a collaborative experience.

#### 5. Transparent Pricing
Clear, affordable pricing with a generous free tier, unlike competitors who hide features behind expensive premium plans.
